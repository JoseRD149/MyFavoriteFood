Canelones

Delicious Homemade Canelones

Ingredients

  • 12 pasta sheets
  • 500g ground beef
  • 200g ricotta cheese
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400ml tomato sauce
  • 100g grated cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Time

Preparation: 30 mins
Cooking: 40 mins

Preparation

1. Cook the pasta sheets in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Drain and set aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© the onion and garlic until soft.
3. Add ground beef to the pan, cook until browned, and season with salt and pepper.
4. Mix the cooked meat with ricotta cheese in a bowl.
5. Fill each pasta sheet with the mixture and roll into canelones.
