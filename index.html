Canelones Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Time

⏱ 0:00
⏳ 0:00

PREPARATION

1

Cook the pasta sheets in boiling water for 2-3 minutes. Drain and set aside.

2

Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion and garlic until soft.

3

Add ground beef, cook until browned, and season with salt and pepper.

4

Mix the cooked meat with ricotta cheese, fill the pasta sheets, and roll into canelones.
